Employment Opportunities

The DNMB is a fantastic career choice for a patriotic American, offering high entry-level salaries, biological immortality, world-class public health insurance, and total mastery over lesser forest creatures.

All applicants to DNMB positions must qualify for a United States security clearance and be certified Uncontaminated. The DNMB is an equal-opportunity employer and does not discriminate on basis of race, gender identity, sexuality, flesh purity, height, moral alignment, or whether or not applicant is on fire. Mutants need not apply.
