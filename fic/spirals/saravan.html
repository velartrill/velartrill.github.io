Saravan

a serial by Lexi Summer Hale
  • The People: totalitarians on a mission to a lawless hellhole.
    1. Cultural Exchange, in which a pair of toughs learns the most important lesson of life on the Saravan streets
    2. Match, in which spies blow off steam the two ways they know how
  • Addicted: the desperate meet the dangerous.
    1. Seduction, in which a lifelong misfit finally finds a home.
    2. Home, in which a battered woman returns to an intoxicating abuser she can’t find any way to live without.
    3. Once a Junkie, Always a Junkie: sometimes people have deeper claws than any drug.
