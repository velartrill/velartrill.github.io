Speaking Beladanese: Vocabulary

by Lexi Summer Hale (@velartrill)

This is a list of Beladanese words. To learn how to put them together to form meaningful sentences, see Speaking Beladanese: Chapter I and following chapters.

Key: "n" means a noun, "v" means a verb, "p" means a postposition, and "d" means a descriptive verb.

A

  • adari, p: among
  • anasto, n: heart
  • ar, n: mother
  • arbara, n: "bastard" (motherless)
  • arbara, d: motherless
  • arche, d: maternal
  • arga, v: to give birth
  • argari, n: voice

B

  • barga, n: ass
  • bargatinga, n: asshole
  • bosta, v: own
  • bostam, v: owner
  • bostagan, n: slave

C

  • cambe, n: tree
  • cambeche, n: arboreal
  • cambedin, n: grass
  • cambescan, n: forest
  • carda, v: do
  • cardast, n: fashion
  • cardastache, d: fashionable
  • cardastagan, n: fashionista
  • chomassara, n: to converse, engage in idle chatter
  • chísa, v: to say, declare
  • chira v: lack
  • chirasta, p: without, lacking
  • corde, d: dead
  • cordeche, d: mortal
  • cordechem, n: mortal
  • cordestue, n: death, mortality
  • corma, n: unmarried man
  • cuenda, n: glass
  • carnag, n-ue: mud
  • cuore, v: expect
  • core, p: inside, within, emerging from
  • cuiona, v: hurt, harm, upset

E

  • é, pron.: this, it
  • ericha, v: happen

F

  • fal, n: daughter
  • farista, v: love (sexually)
  • frema, v: to hear
  • falbara, d: childless
  • faltig, n: tomboy
  • fena, n: sister

G

  • gabra, n: goat
  • gabragan, n: goatherd
  • gelisua, d: happy
  • guarda, v: prove, demonstrate
  • guardam, v: proof, demonstration

M

  • mecua, v: want
  • meclista, n: beauty
  • meclistache, d: beautiful

P

  • parna, v: fuck
  • parna, n: sex
  • parnagan, n: sex worker
  • penda, v: know
  • perande, v. show sth. off
  • puen, n: fire
  • puenche, d: on fire
  • panchende, v: catch fire
  • puenhana, n: firecraft
  • puengan, n: firecrafter

S

  • selacte, v: spank, subjugate

H

  • halla, n: river
  • hacara, v/n: rule
  • hacarabara, d: anarchic
  • hacarabaragan, n: anarchist
  • hacarabarastue, n: anarchy
  • hacaram, v: queen, mistress
  • hacarast, n: kingdom
  • hacaragan, n: subject (of a ruler)
  • hali, n: elf
  • hana, v: create
  • hanast, n: art, creation
  • hanagan, n: artist, scientist
  • hanagan puen, n: physicist
  • hara, v: come

P

  • pendua, n: family

R

  • rafta, v: cause
  • raftast, v: result
  • ramma, v: be inside of
  • rahod, n: shit

S

  • sachora, n: passion, lust
  • sigara, v: beat, discipline
  • suleche, n: a crisp pastry made from seaweed

T

  • tama, n: brother
  • tela, v: meet
  • teliche, v: please
  • tig, n: son
  • tigal, n: father, married man
  • tigalche, d: paternal
  • tigalga, v: sire
  • tinga, n: mouth
